Grilled vegetable salads are a culinary delight found beyond the borders of Tunisia. In the Caucasus, for instance, a similar dish graces the tables, albeit with its own unique flair. Here, vegetables are skewered, roasted over open flames, and then expertly chopped into a harmonious starter mix. The ingredients mirror those of Tunisian cuisine, featuring tomatoes, peppers, onions, and garlic, with eggplants as an optional addition. However, the Tunisian rendition stands out with the inclusion of tuna fish and boiled eggs, elevating it to a heartier meal option.

In the Caucasus region, grilled vegetable salads are not uncommon either. Local cookbooks often feature recipes suggesting a similar approach, albeit with a twist.
